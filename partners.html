The real problem

Most donors do not disappear because they stopped caring.

They disappear because nobody showed them what happened.

You already do meaningful work. The problem is not the mission. The problem is that your team is too lean to package every need well, follow up with every donor, and keep trust alive after the gift.

That creates pressure, inconsistency, and a donor list that never compounds.

What Glimpse does

The proof loop for lean nonprofits.

We help you do three things:

  1. 01 Tell the story clearly.
  2. 02 Package real needs donors can understand instantly.
  3. 03 Make sure every donor gets visible proof and ongoing updates.

What makes this different

Glimpse is additive to the fundraising you already do.

Glimpse does not replace your existing donors, campaigns, or fundraising systems. We help you package needs clearly, run proof loops consistently, and keep donor trust alive after the gift.

In the early phase, the first funded needs can come from Derrick's network, partner-led outreach, and aligned crypto-native supporters. Over time, the goal is to prove Glimpse can open an additional donor channel. That is a hypothesis we validate together, not a promise we assume on day one.

  • Zero disruption to existing fundraising
  • An additional proof-backed donation channel layered on top of your current work
  • White-glove story capture, need packaging, and donor follow-up included
  • Every donor gets updates. No one gives into a black hole.

The guarantee

If the proof loop does not work,
you stay free.

If, after full onboarding and active participation in the founding pilot, Glimpse does not help you fund the first real needs and establish a working proof loop, you stay free beyond the pilot. You keep the assets, packaging, and systems we built together.

This is for you if:

  • You run a lean direct-service nonprofit
  • You have recurring, tangible needs that can be priced and documented
  • Your donor follow-up is weaker than it should be
  • Your team is stretched thin and needs to feel lighter, not busier
  • You can move quickly with founder or ED-level buy-in

This is not for:

  • Policy and advocacy organizations
  • Research-heavy organizations
  • Large institutions with mature development teams
  • Privacy-heavy organizations where proof is hard to document safely
  • Organizations that only want software and no real partnership
