How it works

Clear all the way through.

From the wallet to the proof loop, every step stays visible.

  1. 01
    Connect your walletOpen Glimpse on your Seeker or any Android with a wallet app that supports Mobile Wallet Adapter.
  2. 02
    Pick a needBrowse real needs from nonprofit partners. Each one has a specific price and a clear outcome.
  3. 03
    Enter USDC and signYour wallet handles the signing. The donation is sent directly with a permanent on-chain record.
  4. 04
    Get permanent proofA Solana Memo records the donation on-chain. Wallet, amount, timestamp. A durable receipt the donor can return to later.
  5. 05
    Follow the impactA conversation opens between you and the nonprofit. Real updates. Not a templated email.

What makes it work

Built different on purpose.

Glimpse is not a charity shopping mall. It is infrastructure for trust between donors and nonprofits.

  • On-chain receipts — wallet, amount, timestamp. Immutable. Publicly verifiable by anyone.
  • Direct giving — the donation goes straight to the nonprofit wallet with no platform detour.
  • Real connection — a conversation opens after every donation. Not a mass email. A direct line.
  • Solana — permanent public ledger, fast settlement, and a clean record of what happened.
  • Direct to nonprofit — donations go to the nonprofit's own wallet. Glimpse never holds donor funds.
