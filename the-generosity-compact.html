THE GENEROSITY COMPACT

Generosity is contagious — real impact, real connections, permanent on-chain proof.

Glimpse is building the infrastructure that makes generosity as efficient as commerce. Zero fees to donors. Every donation verified on Solana. Every dollar accounted for, permanently.

This compact sets forth how Glimpse operates, how it is governed, and what it owes to every person who participates. It is the public record and founding agreement of the Glimpse community.

Ratified March 2026 — Solana

01 — The Problem

Billions of dollars flow into charitable giving every year. The system that moves it is slow, opaque, and disconnected from the people it claims to serve.

Donors give once and disappear — not because they stopped caring, but because nobody showed them what happened next. Nonprofits are forced to perform instead of doing the work they set out to do. The best marketers take money from less savvy founders who only focus on solving problems.

There is no permanent record. No verifiable proof. No connection between the person who gave and the person whose life changed.

What would keep someone giving again? The connection piece. Not a templated email blast — something real.

02 — The Solution

Glimpse is a global network of hubs — creator-operators contracted like athletes, compensated by performance — who identify recipients, move donor dollars on-chain, and generate permanent proof of impact.

Speed, trust, and on-chain record. Every time. Everywhere.

This is only possible because of Solana — the only chain fast enough and cheap enough to make micro-donations viable at global scale — and Solana Seeker — a mobile device built for exactly this: putting the giving infrastructure in the hands of hub operators and donors on the ground.

The tech is not incidental. Solana is the reason the north star is achievable.

How It Works

Donor gives USDC through the Glimpse app. The Foundation receives and routes 100% of the donation. An on-chain receipt is created permanently on Solana. A conversation opens between the donor and the Glimpse team, who relay impact updates from the recipient. Zero fees. Full transparency.

03 — Two Entities, One Mission

Glimpse operates through two legally separate but mission-aligned entities. This separation is load-bearing for both tax-exempt status and regulatory compliance.

Glimpse Foundation

501(c)(3) Nonprofit
  • Receives and routes 100% of donations
  • Vets recipients — no prior donor connection
  • Issues on-chain receipts for all transactions
  • Operates lean — zero fee to donors
  • Tax-deductible status pending 501(c)(3) approval

Glimpse PBC

Public Benefit Corporation (For-Profit)
  • Builds and owns the app and technology
  • Stated public benefit = supporting the Foundation
  • Monetizes via cause marketing with local businesses
  • Owns all content produced from campaigns (with consent)
  • Can legally donate to Foundation
Key

These entities operate independently with arm's-length contracts. The PBC cannot control the Foundation. The Foundation cannot distribute profits to the PBC. Separate boards, separate EINs. No single person controls both sides — the Foundation will have an independent board as required by the IRS.

04 — Revenue Model

Donors pay nothing. The Foundation's operational costs are covered campaign-by-campaign through sponsor restricted gifts. The Foundation does not rely on grants.

The PBC operates as a cause marketing agency for local and regional businesses — restaurants, roofers, home services, any business that wants to invest in their community and get credit for it.

What Partners Receive

Engagement: 3–6 month retainers and project-based contracts.

Deliverables: Campaign strategy tied to real charitable impact in their community. Content creation — donation moments, recipient stories, impact proof. Social distribution on Glimpse channels. Crafted offers and discounts explaining why the company invested and what it means to them.

Value: Positive brand awareness backed by on-chain proof of real impact, plus lead and revenue generation. This replaces ineffective local ad spend with story-driven marketing tied to verified community outcomes.
