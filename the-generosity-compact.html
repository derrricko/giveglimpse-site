The Generosity Compact

Every dollar tracked.
Every impact proven.

This compact sets forth how Glimpse operates, what it owes to donors and nonprofit partners, and what claims it is willing to make in public. It is a public record of the standards Glimpse is choosing to hold itself to.

Ratified March 2026 — Solana

01 — THE PROBLEM

The system is broken.

Billions of dollars flow into charitable giving every year. The system that moves it is slow, opaque, and disconnected from the people it claims to serve.

Donors give once and disappear — not because they stopped caring, but because nobody showed them what happened next. Nonprofits are forced to perform instead of doing the work they set out to do.

There is no permanent record. No verifiable proof. No connection between the person who gave and the person whose life changed.

02 — THE SOLUTION

Infrastructure for trust.

Glimpse is the infrastructure that makes generosity as efficient as commerce. Every donation moves on-chain. Every dollar is accounted for. Every outcome is documented permanently.

When a donor gives through Glimpse, the donation goes directly to the nonprofit's own Solana wallet. Zero fees. An on-chain receipt is created permanently. A conversation opens between the donor and the nonprofit. Real updates. Real proof.

Glimpse never holds donor funds. Nonprofits maintain their own wallets and their own keys.

03 — ONE ENTITY, ONE MISSION

Glimpse is a Public Benefit Corporation.

Glimpse is registered in Delaware as a Public Benefit Corporation. Stated public benefit: making charitable giving transparent and verifiable.

Glimpse builds the donor-facing software and founder-delivered proof-loop systems that help nonprofit partners package needs, receive donations, and follow up with proof. Glimpse is a technology vendor and white-glove partner, not a custodian. Donations go directly to each nonprofit's own Solana wallet.

Each nonprofit partner maintains their own tax status. Glimpse is not a nonprofit, not a 501(c)(3), and not a registered charity. Donations made through Glimpse are not tax-deductible through Glimpse itself — donors should consult the nonprofit partner regarding their tax status.

04 — HOW GLIMPSE SUSTAINS ITSELF

Donors never pay. The founding pilot is free.

Zero platform fees on donations. Not reduced fees. Zero. Every dollar a donor sends goes directly to the nonprofit.

The current public offer is the Founding Five pilot: five founding nonprofit partners, 12 months free, and white-glove setup included. Pricing for ongoing service will be finalized after the founding pilot proves the proof loop works in the real world.

Glimpse is not selling cause-marketing campaigns or replacing a nonprofit's existing donor systems. The current offer is focused on story capture, need packaging, proof collection, and donor follow-up.

05 — CURRENT PUBLIC CLAIMS

What Glimpse is willing to promise in public right now.

Glimpse does not claim that broad donor acquisition is already proven. In the early phase, the first funded needs can come from Derrick's network, partner-led outreach, and aligned supporters while the proof loop is being validated.

Glimpse does claim three things clearly: donors never pay platform fees, donations are recorded on-chain, and every donor should receive visible proof and ongoing updates.

Truth-alignment note: Any future disclosures about pricing beyond the founding pilot, digital asset mechanics, or other expansion plans will be published separately after legal and operational review. They are not part of the current donor or partner offer.
06 — COMMITMENTS

What Glimpse owes.

To donors:

  • Zero fees on every donation. Always.
  • A permanent, verifiable on-chain receipt for every transaction.
  • A real conversation with the nonprofit after every gift.
  • Proof of what your money did.

To nonprofits:

  • An additive proof-backed donation channel layered on top of your current fundraising.
  • White-glove story capture, need packaging, and proof loop management.
  • 100% of donation funds go to your wallet. Glimpse never touches them.
  • Your keys, your wallet, your tax status. We are a vendor, not a custodian.

To the community:

  • Transparency in operations, finances, and public claims.
  • Honest communication about what is working and what is not.
  • No fake data, no inflated metrics, no unverified claims.
