Documenting Kindness

A USDC donation app where every dollar is recorded on Solana. Zero fees. Real updates from real people.

What Glimpse Does

Zero Fees

Every dollar goes toward helping people in need. No platform cut.

On-Chain Proof

Every donation recorded on Solana. Verifiable. Permanent.

Real Updates

A message thread opens. You follow the impact of your gift.

How It Works
1
Connect your Seeker wallet
2
Pick a campaign and enter any USDC amount
3
Add context and a note for the recipient
4
Review the details and sign
5
Follow real updates in your message thread
