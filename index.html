How It Works I Run a Nonprofit

Zero Fees

Every dollar goes forward. No platform cut. Not 3%. Not 2.9% + 30 cents. Zero. GoFundMe takes three percent. We take nothing.

On-Chain Proof

Every donation recorded permanently on Solana. Wallet, amount, timestamp. Verifiable by anyone. Immutable. The receipt can never be altered or deleted.

Real Connection

A conversation opens after every donation. Not a templated email. Real updates from the nonprofit. You see exactly what your money did.

The proof loop

Show people what happened, and they give again.

New donor retention is 7.2%. But once a donor gives a second gift, retention jumps to 59%. The second gift is the inflection point. Proof is what triggers it.

  1. 01 Donor gives USDC. Zero fees.
  2. 02 Transaction recorded permanently on Solana.
  3. 03 Impact documented with proof.
  4. 04 Donor sees proof.
  5. 05 Donor gives again.
